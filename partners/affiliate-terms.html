For a sale to qualify for commission, the user must complete a purchase using the affiliate’s assigned promo code, the payment must be successfully collected, and the transaction must not be refunded, reversed, charged back, fraudulent, suspicious, or otherwise invalid.

Sales must be generated by real, independent users. If BlockerMax determines that a transaction was created through collusion, pressure, hidden incentives, artificial arrangements, or any other non-independent behavior between the affiliate and the buyer, the related commission may be voided.
